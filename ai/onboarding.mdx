Layup's AI-powered onboarding creates personalized introduction experiences for new users. By guiding users to key features and helping them achieve initial success, effective onboarding significantly improves adoption, reduces time-to-value, and increases long-term retention.

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="User Segmentation">
    Identify different user types based on roles, goals, or experience levels to create targeted
    onboarding paths.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Experience Design">
    Craft multi-step sequences that progressively introduce your application's core value and
    functionality.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Intelligent Delivery">
    Present onboarding at optimal moments using a mix of welcome tours, feature spotlights, and
    contextual guidance.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Progress Tracking">
    Monitor user advancement through onboarding steps and adapt subsequent experiences based on
    completion status.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Business Impact

- **Faster Time-to-Value**: Help users become productive with your product in less time
- **Increased Adoption**: Users who complete onboarding use more features and engage more deeply
- **Reduced Churn**: Properly onboarded users are less likely to abandon your product
- **Lower Support Costs**: Proactively address common questions before users need to ask

## Configuration Options

Onboarding flows can be customized through the [Layup Dashboard](https://app.layuplabs.ai/onboarding):

- **Multi-path Journeys**: Create different onboarding experiences for various user segments
- **Targeting Rules**: Define when and to whom onboarding is shown
- **Progress Requirements**: Set completion criteria for each step
- **Appearance Settings**: Customize the look and feel to match your brand
