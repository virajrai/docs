## What is Layup?

[Layup](https://layuplabs.ai) is an AI-powered assistance platform that creates interactive guides, answers questions, and provides personalized support—all directly inside your product. When users get stuck, Layup shows them exactly what to do using our proprietary Second Cursor technology.
